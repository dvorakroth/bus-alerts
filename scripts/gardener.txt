a service alert can be identified with:

national level (agency_id==1, no aramaic translation, no cities defined)
agencies (has agency_id, no aramaic translation)
region (agency_id==1, aramaic is region=XX,XX:YY,YY:ZZ,ZZ;)
city (agency_id==1, hebrew contains "hahoda'a relewantit layeshuvim:")
stops (has stop_id, no route_id)
stop-on-route (has stop_id, has route_id, might have aramaic "add_stop_id")
routes-at-stop (has multiple sets of route_id+stop_id, no aramaic translation)
trips-of-route (has trip_id+route_id+schedule_relationship)
